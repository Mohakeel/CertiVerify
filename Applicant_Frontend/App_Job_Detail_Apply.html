The Role

CertiVerify is seeking a highly analytical Senior Verification Analyst to join our Integrity team. In this role, you will be at the front lines of digital identity and credential security. You'll bridge the gap between traditional notary processes and modern cryptographic verification, ensuring that every document that passes through our portal meets the highest standards of authenticity.

Key Responsibilities

  • Execute complex multi-stage verification protocols for international academic and professional credentials.
  • Collaborate with the AI Engineering team to refine automated document processing and fraud detection models.
  • Serve as the final escalation point for high-stakes verification discrepancies.
  • Maintain detailed audit logs in compliance with global security standards.

Requirements

Education

Bachelor's or Master's in Data Science, Law, or a related field.

Experience

5+ years in forensic analysis, KYC/AML, or background verification.

Tech Skills

Proficiency with SQL, blockchain explorers, and document metadata analysis.

Soft Skills

Unwavering attention to detail and radical integrity.
